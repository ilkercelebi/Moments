How do I add a widget to my Home Screen?
Long press on an empty area of your Home Screen until the apps jiggle. Tap the "+" button in the top left corner, search for "Moments", and select your preferred widget size.
How do I restore my purchases?
Go to the Settings page within the app and tap on "Restore Purchases". Ensure you are logged in with the same Apple ID used to make the purchase.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely. Moments uses iCloud to sync your data, meaning it is encrypted and accessible only by you. We do not have access to your personal events.
