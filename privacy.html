Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 17, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Moments. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

2. Data Collection

Moments is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share your personal event data on our servers. All your reminders and event details are stored locally on your device and synced securely via your personal iCloud account.

3. Usage Data

We may collect anonymous, aggregated usage data to help us improve the app's performance and stability. This data does not identify you personally.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services like RevenueCat for processing subscriptions. These services adhere to their own privacy policies and security standards.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@moments.app.
